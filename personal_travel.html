Where am I?

Most of the time I am supposed to be in Concepción, where I live and work, or near it (let's say within a 200 km radius or so). Nevertheless, being an astronomer requires me to travel around the country (for observing trips, for example) or around the world (for conferences, collaboration visits, etc.). Below you can find a list of my already scheduled travel plans. These can vary sometimes with a short notice, and I will make my best effort to try to keep the trip list (see below) up to date. Please visit this page regularly to know whether I am going to be around, in case you want to visit me at home or in the office. If in doubt, please e-mail me!
